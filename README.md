**Energy-Luxury Research: Oil Shocks & European Luxury Giants (2019–2022)**

_Analyzing the impact of oil price volatility on LVMH and Kering’s financial performance._


📌 Project Overview

This repository explores how oil price shocks between 2019 and 2022—driven by geopolitical events (e.g., COVID-19, Russia-Ukraine war) and supply chain disruptions—affected the stock prices, revenue, and profitability of two European luxury conglomerates:





LVMH (Moët Hennessy Louis Vuitton)



Kering (Gucci, Saint Laurent, Bottega Veneta)

The analysis bridges energy economics and luxury finance, with a focus on:
✔ Correlation between Brent crude oil prices and luxury stock performance.
✔ Sector-specific resilience: How luxury goods (as "discretionary" spending) weather energy crises.
✔ Comparative insights: LVMH vs. Kering’s exposure to energy costs (e.g., logistics, raw materials).
